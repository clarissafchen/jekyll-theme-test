About

Hi! I am Clarissa Fonseca. I was born in Mexico in 1990 and moved to U.S. in 2003. I made it my goal to have a career in science throughout highschool, got my B.A. in Biology in 2012 and headed off to med school in 2013. As much as I love science, I realized that was not my passion, graphic design is. I have always been enthralled by art, and found joy in making my own through painting and doodles, and now through digital art. I am an extremely motivated individual and I work hard in honing my skills as a graphic designer and now love what I do. I make things pretty and simple.
